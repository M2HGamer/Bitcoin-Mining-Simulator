FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bitcoin Mining?
Bitcoin mining is the process of validating and recording transactions on the Bitcoin blockchain by solving complex cryptographic puzzles using computer hardware.
Is this real Bitcoin mining?
No, this is a simulated mining experience designed to help you understand how mining works. No real Bitcoin is generated or spent.
How does the Bitcoin balance update?
For each simulated mining point, your Bitcoin balance is incremented slightly, giving you the feel of earning Bitcoin through mining.
